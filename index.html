About Me

Bonjour! I'm Venkat Vivek, an Industrial Engineering graduate from the University of Toronto born and raised in the charming capital city of Ontario, Canada with a passion for AI, ML and all things data! I wanted to showcase my skills and curiosities in a fun and interactive way through passion projects and written thought pieces.

With over three years of experience in Machine Learning and Data Analytics, I've worked on Marketing Mixed Modelling (MMM), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), Customer Churn Prediction, Support Ticket Sentiment Analysis, and more! My current focus is on strengthening my foundational knowledge in deployment orchestration and productionizing models at scale on the cloud.

When I'm not working with data, you can find me taking mental health walks, bird watching, reading psychological thrillers, watching sports, SNL and Survivor or volunteering at the Hands-On-Biodiversity exhibit at the ROM.
